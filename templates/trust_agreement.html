trust agreement

4orm is a community-driven platform where users can customize their profiles and pages with custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

This means the site operates on a foundation of trust. We maintain regular backups so we can revert any damage, but we ask that you respect the community:

  • Don't insert malicious scripts that steal data or harm other users
  • Don't intentionally break other users' experience
  • Don't abuse shared spaces (forum, guestbooks) with spam or harmful content

If you break this trust, your account will be removed from the site.
